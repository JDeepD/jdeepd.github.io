[GSoC] Week 2: Getting it all together

June 9, 2022 2-minute read
GSoC

This week was a bit engaging(probably the most engaging till now). So, I had to start off where Hariom left. His work already ported most of the pretty formats to use ref-filter’s logic. But I couldn’t just start off from that branch directly since its more than a year old. A lot of changes have happened since then.

So first, I had to rebase pretty-lib-2.0.3 onto current master. I pulled that branch and began rebasing to master. Well, I got rebase conflicts(which was expected) but when you get rebase conflicts on a codebase where you donot have a good track of its history, things become messy. I would suggest if you ever have to resolve conflicts, prefer a GUI(Gitkraken or something similar) over TUI/text editor. I tried resolving those conflicts using just a text editor and it was horrible. I spent almost 3 hours messing something up, aborting, trying again. It was tedious but I later used Gitkraken. It still took time to resolve the conflicts manually but it was far less than what I would have spent if I resolved them on a text editor.
