[GSoC] Week 0: Community Bonding Period

May 23, 2022 One-minute read
JDeepD
GSoC

So, My proposal to the org git got accepted for GSoC 2022. For the next few days, I will be exploring the codebase, also try to make some side contributions under the mentorship of Christian and Hariom. I am already in contact with them regarding the proposal.

Till now, I have read most the “getting started” docs in Hacking Git. Yesterday, I was reading this very interesting tutorial on Write yourself a Git. Its basically a prototype implementation of git in Python. Currently, the git codebase is too large to explore everything from scratch so this tutorial gives some insight on just the core functionality and working of git.

This week, I have following targets:

  1. Complete the tutorial asap.
  2. Go through the previous works of Hariom, ZheNing and Olga.
  3. If possible, lurk into some beginner patches and try to review them.

I will pushing all my work to my git fork on Github. Will update about the branch later.

Overall, I am pretty excited to work for such a popular and reputed open-source project.

Will be posting weekly :) on updates/difficulties and solutions that I face during the journey.

Till then, Signing off.
